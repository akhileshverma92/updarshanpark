About UP Darshan Park

Welcome to Lucknow's first waste-to-wonder park, 'UP Darshan Park'. This innovative park showcases the perfect blend of sustainability and cultural heritage, featuring miniature replicas of 16 iconic monuments from Uttar Pradesh. Each masterpiece is crafted using recycled materials, demonstrating our commitment to environmental conservation while celebrating our rich architectural legacy.

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism and environmental awareness while preserving our cultural heritage. Through creative upcycling, we've transformed industrial and household waste into magnificent replicas that tell the story of Uttar Pradesh's architectural grandeur.

Our Vision & Mission

Inspired by our success with Bharat Darshan Park in Delhi, we embarked on this ambitious project to create UP Darshan Park. Our vision extends beyond just creating a tourist attraction - we aim to demonstrate how waste can be transformed into wonderful works of art and architecture.

Our dedicated team of artists and craftsmen have meticulously created these replicas using various types of waste materials, including iron scraps, vehicle parts, old machinery, and household metal waste. Each monument tells a story of innovation, sustainability, and cultural preservation.
