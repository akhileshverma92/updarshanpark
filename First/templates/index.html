Welcome To UP DARSHAN PARK

Welcome to Lucknow's first waste-to-wonder park, 'UP Darshan Park'. This park features small replicas of 16 famous monuments from the state, constructed using waste materials such as iron scraps, scraps of four-wheelers, rolling shutters, rusted almirah, tyre rims, bicycles frames and household iron waste, among others.

The models of monuments include Bara Imambara, Lucknow Vidhan Sabha, Jhansi Fort, Banke Bihari and Vrindavan temples of Mathura, Devipatan Mandir of Balrampur, Taj Mahal, Kashi Vishwanath Temple of Varanasi and Dudhwa National Park. Besides, an open-air theatre, food court and other facilities will also be part of the park.
